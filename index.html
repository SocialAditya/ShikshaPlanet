Editorial Board

Shiksha Planet Editorial Board

The Shiksha Planet Editorial Board is a dedicated team of eminent educators, music scholars, and subject matter experts committed to excellence in teacher recruitment exams. With deep-rooted expertise in Indian Classical Music and a profound understanding of competitive exam patterns—including KVS, NVS, EMRS, BPSC TRE, DSSSB, UP TGT/PGT, CUET, UGC NET, and all Music Teacher Recruitment and Entrance Exams—the board focuses on creating high-quality, research-based content.

The 'Music MCQs Masterbook' is the result of an extensive analysis of previous years' papers and the latest patterns of various competitive exams, ensuring that every aspirant has the most relevant and accurate resource to achieve their dream career.
